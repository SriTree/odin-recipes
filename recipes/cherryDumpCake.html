Chocolate Cherry Dump Cake

Description

This 3-ingredient chocolate cherry dump cake could not be easier! Just pie filling, chocolate cake mix, and butterâ€”you'll have it ready to bake before you can say, 'preheated oven'.

Ingredients

  • 2 (21 ounce) cans cherry pie filling
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) box chocolate cake mix
  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick), cut into 16 slices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Pour pie filling into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Spread cake mix evenly over the pie filling, taking care not to mix it into the fruit. Top evenly with butter slices.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden brown and bubbly, about 45 minutes. Let stand at least 5 minutes before serving.
